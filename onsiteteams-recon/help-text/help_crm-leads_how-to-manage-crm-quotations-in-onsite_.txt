Before You Start The client must be added as a party before they can be selected on a quotation Add items to the Rate Library in advance for faster quotation creation Configure Terms and Conditions in Settings if you want default terms to pre-fill on quotations Accessing Quotations Click CRM in the left sidebar, then click the Quotation tab. Quotations can also be created from within a lead: open the lead, click the Quotation tab, and click + New Quotation . This links the quotation to the lead automatically. Items added below a section become sub-items (2.1, 2.2, etc.) under that section heading. Use sections to organise large quotations by trade or scope. Clients find sectioned quotations significantly easier to read and approve. Add an Item Option A â€” From Library: Click + Add Item , then + Add Item from Library . Edit the text to reflect your payment terms, change request policy, validity period, or any contractual notes specific to this quotation. Default terms from Settings are pre-filled if configured. A quotation with sections for Civil, Electrical, Plumbing, and Interior is much easier for a client to review than a flat list of 40 items. Set Tax Type before saving. Item Level Tax applies different rates per item â€” use it for mixed-trade quotations. Bill Level Tax applies one rate to the total â€” use it for single-scope work. Add items from the Rate Library for consistency and speed. Library items carry pre-set cost and selling prices, so you only need to enter the quantity. Check the Markup before sharing. The financial summary shows total Cost Price and Markup. Send the quotation only when the margin is within your target. Move status to Client Confirmed once approved. This makes it easy to filter all confirmed quotations and convert them to projects. Create quotations from within the lead record, not from the Quotations tab directly, whenever possible. This keeps the lead and quotation linked for pipeline tracking.
