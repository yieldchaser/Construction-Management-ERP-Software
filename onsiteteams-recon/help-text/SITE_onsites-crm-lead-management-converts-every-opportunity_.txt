What CRM Means in a Construction Context? CRM in construction does not end at contact database. In simple terms, it turns sales activity into something measurable and manageable. The Construction Lead Journey Explained Most construction leads follow a pattern that is easy to follow. It all starts with a question, which can come from a phone call, a form on a website, or a recommendation. The lead is then qualified to know the scope, budget, and deadlines. Then there are site visits and technical talks. Proposals and estimates are made, changed, and looked over. Before a lead is turned into a project or closed, there are negotiations. When each of these steps is written down properly, teams can understand how far along each chance is.
