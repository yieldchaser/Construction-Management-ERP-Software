What is the TDS rate for Onsite subscription payments? Onsite subscription payments fall under Section 194J of the Income Tax Act (fees for technical services). The applicable TDS rate is 10%. Deduct TDS on the base invoice amount before GST and deposit it to the government. Provide your TDS certificate to the Onsite team after deduction.
