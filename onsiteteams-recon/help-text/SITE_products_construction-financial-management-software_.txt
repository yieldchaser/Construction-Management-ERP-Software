Industry Challenges What Happens Without Digital Real-Time Financial Control Materials, labour, and subcontractors form 60-80% of project costs. Without live tracking, profit margins shrink silently with no early warning. Most contractors rely on Tally or Excel where BOQs are made once and never tracked against actual spending, leading to 15-30% cost overruns. Budget Deviations Go Unnoticed BOQs sit as static PDFs while site expenses flow untracked. Material consumption, labour costs, and subcontractor bills exceed planned amounts, but deviations surface only at month end when margins have already eroded. Billing Cycles Delay Collections RA bill generation takes days because progress tracking and billing systems stay disconnected. Retention tracking, advance adjustments, and milestone billing happen manually, choking cashflow and delaying payments. Profit Known Too Late Tally tracks company accounts but cannot show project-wise profitability. You cannot tell which projects make money and which burn cash until final reconciliation when corrective action is impossible. Cost Leakage Drains Profits Without project-wise allocation, fuel costs, petty cash, equipment hire, and overheads get misallocated or lost completely. Duplicate vendor bills clear approvals. Site expenses recorded on WhatsApp never reach your accounting system.
