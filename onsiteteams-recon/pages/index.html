Take Complete Control of your Projects with Onsite

Managing construction projects means juggling deadlines, budgets, and resources often across multiple sites. Onsite gives you a unified platform to plan, execute, and monitor everything from one place.

Multi Level Approvals

Every purchase request, vendor bill, subcontractor payment, and site expense goes through a defined approval chain before any commitment is made. No bypassing. No verbal authorizations that leave no record. Every approval decision is logged with the approver name, timestamp, and action taken so management always knows who authorized what and when.

Multi Site Visibility from One Dashboard

Owners and directors see project health, labour attendance, procurement status, and financial performance across every active site from one dashboard without calling individual project managers for updates. Problems surface through the system before they reach clients, giving management enough lead time to intervene before delays and cost overruns compound.

Budget vs Actuals in Real Time

Every material receipt, labour wage, subcontractor bill, and site expense updates project cost data the moment it is recorded at site. Finance teams and project managers see the same live numbers without waiting for end-of-month reconciliation. Budget overruns surface as they develop, not after the damage is already done and recovery is no longer possible.

Finance Connected to Operations

In most construction companies, finance teams work from invoices and bank statements while site teams work from WhatsApp updates and paper registers. Onsite connects both on the same platform. A material delivery recorded at site flows into procurement, updates inventory, triggers bill matching, and reflects in project P&L without anyone re-entering data between systems.

FAQs

What is Construction Management Software?

Construction Management Software (CMS) is a digital platform designed to plan, execute, and monitor construction projects from start to finish. It manages workflows, schedules, resources, and communication. However, a true Construction ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) goes further. Onsite Teams is a specialized ERP that focuses on cost control by integrating financial data with site activity. This means we track every material indent, labor hour, and procurement cost in real-time a level of detail generic CMS and PM tools cannot match.

What is the best construction management software (CMS) in India?

Onsite is one of India’s leading construction management platforms used by 10,000+ contractors for cost control, project tracking, and accurate billing. Onsite website and app gives the flexibility for your office and field team to collaborate in real time with connected workflow

How can the Onsite benefit construction companies?

The Onsite ERP software streamlines site management, making it effortless to handle multiple sites. It automates labor attendance and salary calculations, saving valuable time and effort. Additionally, it fosters seamless teamwork, boosts productivity, and ensures effective collaboration.

How does project management software for construction help reduce cost?

It prevents wastage, tracks materials, automates billing, and aligns teams reducing project costs by 7–10%.

How is Onsite is different from other construction ERP systems?

Unlike traditional ERP software that is complex and time-consuming to deploy, Onsite is built specifically for construction project management. It offers real-time site updates through a mobile app and advanced planning through a desktop ERP, ensuring faster adoption, easy usability, and end-to-end control of your projects.

Does Onsite work on both mobile and desktop?

Yes. This dual approach ensures that site engineers, project managers, and office teams stay connected and updated at all times.
