Our Story
2021
How Onsite Construction ERP Was Founded
Founded in India. Built for contractors everywhere.
Onsite Teams (legally Abeyaantrix Technology Private Limited) is a construction ERP software company founded in 2021. We observed that Indian construction contractors were managing crore-value projects on WhatsApp groups and paper registers — not from lack of discipline, but from lack of tools built for how they actually work.
Every construction ERP they tried was built for manufacturing or a Western market. The language didn’t match. The workflows didn’t match. The pricing assumed an IT department — which no contractor has. So most gave up and went back to Excel.
“The problem wasn’t that contractors lacked discipline. The problem was that the tools available were designed for someone else. We set out to build the construction management software that should have existed.”
We spent months on actual project sites before writing a line of code. We watched material management happen on paper. We watched labour attendance tracked in registers that got lost. We saw billing disputes happen because there was no single source of truth for subcontractor billing.
Today, Onsite is trusted by 500+ construction companies across India and the Middle East. We are headquartered in Delhi, India and serve clients in UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.
